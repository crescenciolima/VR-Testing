# Test Case – Usability

## Objective

Verify that a high school student can easily understand the purpose of the platform, quickly learn how to use it (navigation, interaction with NPCs, minigames, and quizzes), and operate all functionalities without confusion or excessive effort.

---

## Steps

1. Access the metaverse for the first time through a web browser on a laptop (without prior instructions).
2. Freely explore the environment for 10 minutes, interacting with the guide Maria Felipa, accessing menus, and attempting to play at least one minigame and complete one quiz.
3. Evaluate whether the student can understand:
a. The purpose of the experience (recreating the Battle of Itaparica with a decolonial perspective).
b. How to move around the 3D environment.
c. How to interact with NPCs and objects.
d. How to access and play minigames/quizzes.
4. After the exploration, ask the student to complete a short questionnaire about the ease of use and understanding of the environment.

---

## Expected Result

- The student quickly understands that the environment recreates a historical event using an educational and decolonial approach.
- The student is able to learn, without external assistance, how to use the controls, navigate menus, and interact with the content within 5 minutes.
- The student completes the required tasks (movement, interaction, minigame, and quiz) without recurring questions or signs of frustration.
- The student reports in the questionnaire that they understood the platform’s purpose and found it easy to use.
